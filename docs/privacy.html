Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Caldera Money is a spending clarity app that helps you understand Available to Spend after money has been Set Aside for future needs.

Financial Data

When you use Bank Sync, Caldera Money uses linked account data to estimate balances and Available to Spend. This may include account balances, account names, account types, and transaction data where available from your linked institution. Transaction names or descriptions are used only if they are included in linked account data.

Bank Credentials

Caldera Money does not store your bank login credentials. Bank Sync is handled by Plaid, a financial account connection provider.

Plaid

Caldera Money uses Plaid to connect linked accounts with your consent. Plaid may collect and process information according to Plaid's own policies and user consent flow.

Local App Data

User-created app data, such as Cash Cushion values, Savings Goals, Upcoming Expenses, Timeline entries, and Debt Payoff items, may be stored locally on your device to provide the app's planning features.

Set Aside Money

Set Aside money is virtual planning inside Caldera Money. Your money stays in your bank account. Caldera Money does not move money, make payments, or change real account or debt balances. You should verify real balances, transfers, and payments with your financial institution.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

Caldera Money does not currently use third-party analytics or crash reporting in the app. If that changes, this policy will be updated.

Deletion and Removal Requests

You can disconnect linked banks in the app. If you are signed in, you can also delete your Caldera Money account in the app. For deletion, removal, or privacy support, contact mthomas7546@icloud.com.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to mthomas7546@icloud.com.
