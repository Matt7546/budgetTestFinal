Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Caldera is a personal finance app designed to help users understand cash, protected money, upcoming expenses, debt, and safe-to-spend amounts.

Financial Data

Financial data is used only to power budgeting, account overview, protected-money, upcoming-expense, and safe-to-spend features inside Caldera. This may include account balances, account names, account types, and transaction data where available.

Bank Credentials

Caldera does not store your bank login credentials. Bank linking is handled by Plaid, a secure financial account connection provider.

Plaid

Caldera uses Plaid to securely connect financial accounts. Plaid may collect and process information according to Plaid's own policies and user consent flow.

Local App Data

User-created app data, such as savings goals, reserve amounts, timeline events, and upcoming-expense allocations, may be stored locally on your device to provide the app's budgeting features.

Deletion and Removal Requests

To request deletion, removal, or support with your data, contact mthomas7546@icloud.com.

Contact
