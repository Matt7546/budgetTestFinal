Terms of Use

Last updated: July 6, 2026

These Terms are a plain-language beta draft and are not final attorney-reviewed legal advice. By using Caldera Money, you agree to use it as a planning tool and to verify important financial information with your financial institution.

Planning Tool, Not a Bank

Caldera Money helps you understand Available to Spend after money has been Set Aside for future needs. Caldera Money is not a bank, card issuer, lender, or payment provider.

Set Aside Is Virtual Planning

Set Aside money is planning inside the app. Your money stays in your bank account. Caldera Money does not move money, make payments, transfer funds, or change real account or debt balances.

Bank Sync and Balances

Bank Sync uses linked account data to estimate balances and Available to Spend. Linked account data may be delayed, incomplete, or unavailable. You should verify real balances, due dates, transfers, and payments with your bank, card issuer, lender, or other financial institution.

Debt Payoff

Debt Payoff helps you plan money for card or debt payments. It does not make payments for you and does not reduce your real debt balance. Your real balance changes only when your financial institution processes and reports real activity.

Beta and TestFlight Use

Caldera Money may be offered as beta or TestFlight software. Beta software may contain bugs, incomplete features, delayed account data, or inaccurate information. Do not rely on Caldera Money as your only source for payment timing, account balances, or spending decisions.

No Professional Advice

Caldera Money is not financial, investment, tax, legal, credit, or debt advice. It is designed to help you plan and understand your own spending.

Account Controls

Where supported, you can disconnect linked banks and delete your account from inside the app. If you need help with account removal, privacy requests, bugs, or confusing numbers, contact support.
