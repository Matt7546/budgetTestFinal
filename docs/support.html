What Caldera Money Is

A calm spending clarity app for Available to Spend, Set Aside money, and what is coming.

What It Is Not

Caldera Money does not move money, make payments, or replace your bank or card statement.

Bank Sync

Get help linking accounts through Plaid or refreshing balances. Always verify real balances and payments with your financial institution.

Beta Limitations

During beta, Bank Sync may require manual refreshes and some linked account data may be delayed.

Report a Confusing Number

Include the screen, what looked wrong, and whether your bank data was recently refreshed. Do not send bank passwords or full account numbers.

Privacy Requests

Contact support for bank disconnection, account deletion, removal, or privacy-related questions.
